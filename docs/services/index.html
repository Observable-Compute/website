Educational Services

As a nonprofit organization serving South Dakota and the surrounding areas, the Observable Compute Foundation is dedicated to ensuring that our communities are not left behind in the AI revolution.

We offer on-site educational workshops and classes to teach individuals how to use and understand Large Language Models (LLMs) safely and effectively.

Who We Serve

Classrooms & Educators

We provide tailored curricula for high schools and universities to integrate AI literacy into their programs. We teach students how to use AI as a learning tool rather than a shortcut, emphasizing critical thinking and source evaluation.

Businesses & Facilities

Upgrade your team's technological skills. We offer corporate workshops to help organizations leverage open-source models for workflow automation, data analysis, and secure communications without compromising proprietary data.

Nursing Homes & Community Centers

Technology should be accessible to all ages. We conduct engaging, hands-on sessions at community centers and nursing homes, demonstrating how AI can be used for accessibility, companionship, voice-to-text writing, and staying connected with loved ones.

Schedule a Visit

Are you located in South Dakota or a surrounding area? We are available to travel to your facility to conduct these sessions. Contact us to design a workshop that fits your audience's technical proficiency and interests.
